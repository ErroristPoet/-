In two weeks, I turn 36

November 9th, 2021

I'd lie and say it had recently come to my attention I'm getting old, or older,
but the truth is I'm well aware of the significace of yet another year around the sun.
This year, unlike the last two, we'll be free from the COVID-19 related lockdowns,
and I plan on spending days in celebration with friends playing Board Games.
Specifically Nemesis and Dune: Imperium. Highly excited.
