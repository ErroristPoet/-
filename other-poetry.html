Death is a whore It gets around To all the people Big and small To all the people Young and old The day we do part This very mortal coil May it be kicking and screaming Or lovingly embracing Your very last moments And run through your mind The long and short of your life Death is a whore It deserves all your fear Taking away all your love It deserves all your respect Taking away all your pain It deserves all your ire Taking away all your friends It deserves all your hate Taking away all your family It deserves all your time For you'll soon run out
