The Troubles with viewport height on certain mobiles

November 5th, 2021

I'm looking at you, mobiles with nav bars down the bottom.
Specfically Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra, which is what I have.
I write poems, right, and I like minimalist design for websites.
To that end, I attempted to craft a viewport height for my poetry site intro.
This involved more effort than I thought it would, largely due to the inclusion of said nav bar.
In the end, I used media queries to separate desktop size from mobile size.
Finally, after weeks of commits, pushes and a lack of progeress, I created a new container.
Set the height for the parent container to 95vh, to allow for nav bars and indeed, my site's nav bar.
And also set the media query for small screen's child container to 85vh to allow for room.
Ah, success, how sweet you smell.
