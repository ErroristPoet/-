Learning to Ice Skate

November 4th, 2021

Recently, possibly due to having ADHD, I've picked up a new, expensive hobby.
Much to the delight of my lovely wife.
It happens to be something I'm currently quite passionate about, as it goes with ADHD.
However, I'm equal parts terrified and exhilerated by it.
Prior to last Saturday, I had been to an Ice Rink twice in my life.
But with a bit of luck, a chunk of change and an interest that is bordering on unhealthy,
I signed up for Ice Skating lessons before even setting skate to ice for the third time.
Since then, I've become a tad more confident after my first lesson.
You could say I'm skating on thin ice, but as you'll see below, I prefer to think:
"Slow and steady wins no race, but it keeps the ice from off ya face."
